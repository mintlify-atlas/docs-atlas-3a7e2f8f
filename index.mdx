---
title: "CloudStream"
description: "An extension-based Android media center for streaming and downloading movies, TV shows, anime, and more — ad-free, with full user control."
---

CloudStream is a free, open-source Android app that lets you watch and download media from a wide variety of sources through a powerful extension system. There are no ads, no tracking, and no built-in content — you bring your own sources via extensions.

<Warning>
  By default, CloudStream does not provide any video sources. You must install extensions to add streaming functionality to the app.
</Warning>
