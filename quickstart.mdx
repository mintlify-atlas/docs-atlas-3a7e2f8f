title: "Quick start"
description: "Go from a fresh CloudStream install to watching content in under 5 minutes."
---

This guide walks you through the steps to get content playing as quickly as possible. You will add a repository, install an extension from it, and start browsing.

If you have not installed CloudStream yet, start with the [Installation](/installation) guide first.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open CloudStream">
    Launch CloudStream from your app drawer. On the first run you may be prompted to select a profile — you can skip this or set one up later.

    The home screen will be empty until you install at least one extension.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go to Extensions">
    Tap the **Extensions** icon in the bottom navigation bar, or open the side menu and select **Extensions**.

    This is where you manage all installed extensions and the repositories they come from.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add a repository">
    A repository is a hosted list of extensions. Extensions are community-maintained, so you need to add at least one repository to browse available extensions.

    1. Inside **Extensions**, tap **Repositories** (or the repository icon at the top).
    2. Tap **Add repository** (the `+` icon).
    3. Enter a repository URL and confirm.

    One commonly used community repository is available at `https://recloudstream.github.io/cloudstream-extensions-hexated/`. You can find additional repositories shared by the community.

    <Note>
      The extension ecosystem is entirely community-maintained. CloudStream does not officially endorse any specific extension or repository. Only add repositories from sources you trust, and make sure any content you access complies with the laws in your country.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Install an extension">
    After adding a repository, its extensions appear in the **Extensions** list.

    1. Browse or search for an extension you want to use.
    2. Tap the extension to view its details, then tap **Install**.
    3. Wait for the download to complete. The extension is active immediately — no restart needed.

    You can install as many extensions as you like. Each one adds a new content source to your home screen and search.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse content from the home screen">
    Return to the **Home** screen. You will now see rows of content populated by your installed extensions — featured titles, recently updated episodes, and more.

    Scroll through the categories or tap an extension's section heading to browse its full catalog.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a title, choose quality, and play">
    Tap any title to open its detail page. From there:

    1. Select the season and episode (for series), or go directly to the links (for movies).
    2. Tap a stream link. CloudStream fetches available quality options.
    3. Choose your preferred quality and tap **Play**.

    The built-in player launches and playback begins.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Use the **Search** tab to query all of your installed extensions at once. Results from every source appear together, so you can find content without knowing which extension carries it.
</Tip>

## What to explore next

Once you are set up, you can:

- Connect a tracking service like **AniList** or **MyAnimeList** under **Settings** → **Account** to sync your watch history automatically.
- Download episodes for offline viewing using the download button on any stream link.
- Cast to a TV using the **Chromecast** button in the player.
- Add more extensions from additional repositories to expand your content library.
