---
title: "What is CloudStream"
description: "CloudStream is a free, open-source, extension-based Android media center with no ads and no tracking."
---

CloudStream is an extension-based multimedia player for Android that gives you complete control over what you watch and how you watch it. The app itself ships with no built-in content sources — instead, you install extensions that connect to the sources you choose. There are no ads, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind.

<Warning>
  CloudStream does not provide any video sources out of the box. You must install at least one extension before you can browse or play content. See the [Quick start](/quickstart) guide to get up and running quickly.
</Warning>

## Key features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Extension system" icon="puzzle-piece">
    Install community-built extensions that connect CloudStream to the content sources you want. Add or remove sources at any time without reinstalling the app.

  <Card title="Built-in player" icon="play">
    A full-featured video player with subtitle support, adjustable playback speed, picture-in-picture, and seek bar previews.

  <Card title="Downloads" icon="download">
    Download episodes and movies for offline viewing directly from within the app.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Tracking integration" icon="chart-line">
    Sync your watch history and progress with external tracking services automatically as you watch.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Chromecast" icon="cast">
    Cast content to any Chromecast-compatible device on your network.
  </Card>
  <Card title="TV support" icon="tv">
    Full Android TV and Fire TV support with a lean-back interface optimized for remote navigation.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Supported content types

CloudStream extensions can provide access to a wide range of content formats:

- **Movies**
- **TV series**
- **Anime**
- **Live TV** (via IPTV sources)
- **Audiobooks**
- **Podcasts**
- **Torrents** (via magnet links)

## Tracking services

CloudStream integrates with the following tracking and library services:

- **AniList**
- **MyAnimeList (MAL)**
- **Kitsu**
- **Simkl**
- **Local list** (offline, no account required)
